# Legal and compliance

The **Legal and Compliance** section outlines the regulatory, licensing, and security frameworks governing Claude Code’s usage, ensuring adherence to Anthropic’s policies and external standards. It covers **legal agreements** (distinguishing between **Commercial Terms** for API/enterprise users and **Consumer Terms** for Free/Pro/Max plans), **compliance requirements** (including **BAA extensions for healthcare data** under Zero Data Retention), and **usage policies** (e.g., **acceptable use limits**, **OAuth/API key authentication restrictions**, and prohibitions on credential misuse). Security and trust measures are detailed via Anthropic’s **Trust Center** and **Transparency Hub**, while **vulnerability reporting** channels are provided for responsible disclosure. The page emphasizes **third-party restrictions** (e.g., no routing Free/Pro/Max credentials via SDKs) and **enforcement actions** for violations.
