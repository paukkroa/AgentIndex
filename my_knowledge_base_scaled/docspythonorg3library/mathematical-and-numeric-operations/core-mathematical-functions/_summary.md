The **Core Mathematical Functions** section covers Python’s built-in modules for numerical computations, including the `math` module for real-number operations (constants, trigonometry, exponentials, logarithms, etc.) and the `cmath` module for complex-number calculations (polar conversion, hyperbolic functions, roots, and logarithmic operations). These modules provide essential tools for scientific, engineering, and data analysis workflows, bridging Python’s general-purpose syntax with specialized mathematical requirements.
