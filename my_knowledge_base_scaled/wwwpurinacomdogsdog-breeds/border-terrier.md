# Border Terrier

The **Border Terrier** is a small, high-energy, short-coated breed originally bred in the 18th century for farm stock protection along the Scotland-England border. Males stand **13–16 inches** (females 11–14 inches) and weigh **11.5–15.5 lbs**, with a water-resistant coat requiring **regular brushing and biannual stripping**. Recognized colors include **red, grizzle-and-tan, blue-and-tan, or wheaten**. Known for intelligence, quick learning, and responsiveness to training, they excel in **agility, obedience, and tracking**. Affectionate yet persistent, they thrive with structured activities and mental stimulation. Famous for roles in films (*"There’s Something About Mary," "Anchorman"*), they pair well with active owners and multi-pet homes. Recommended products include high-protein diets (e.g., **Purina ONE**) to support their energy and joint health.
