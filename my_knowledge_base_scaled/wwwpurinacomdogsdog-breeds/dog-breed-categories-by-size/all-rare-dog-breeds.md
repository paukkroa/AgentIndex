# All Rare Dog Breeds

This page curates a selection of **rare and lesser-known dog breeds**, highlighting their exclusivity and unique appeal in the U.S. market. It provides a centralized hub for exploring **eight distinct breeds**, including working hounds (e.g., *American Foxhound*, *English Foxhound*), ancient or specialized herding dogs (*Norwegian Lundehund*, *Pyrenean Shepherd*), and historic terriers (*Skye Terrier*). Each breed’s page offers detailed insights into temperament, history, care requirements, and rarity, catering to enthusiasts seeking uncommon companions. The page emphasizes accessibility via a **filterable list**, enabling users to navigate by breed type or size (e.g., small breeds) for tailored discovery.
