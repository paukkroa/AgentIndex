This section explores **large dog breeds**, detailing their physical characteristics, temperaments, care requirements, and historical significance. From the powerful, double-coated **Akita** to the muscular **American Bulldog**, each breed is examined for size, energy levels, grooming needs, and health considerations. Highlights include the **Arctic sled-dog heritage of the Alaskan Malamute**, the **hunting instincts of the American English Coonhound**, and the **versatile yet loyal American Staffordshire Terrier**, with insights into nutrition, training, and breed-specific health concerns. Whether for companionship, work, or sport, these breeds offer distinct traits suited to active owners.
