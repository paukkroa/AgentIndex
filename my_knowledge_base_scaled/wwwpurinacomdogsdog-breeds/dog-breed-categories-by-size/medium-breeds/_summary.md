The **Medium Breeds** section provides an in-depth exploration of versatile and distinctive canine breeds spanning hunting, companionship, and working roles. It highlights iconic scent-based hounds like Foxhounds, Beagles, and Bassets, alongside hardy Terrier varieties (10+ breeds) known for their bold personalities. The section also covers the low-energy yet affectionate Bulldog, the rare yet agile American Water Spaniel, and breeds celebrated for their striking appearances and temperaments—such as the regal Afghan Hound, the playful Corgi, and the resilient Tibetan Terrier. Each breed’s unique traits, origins, and suitability for different lifestyles are examined to offer comprehensive insights for potential owners.
