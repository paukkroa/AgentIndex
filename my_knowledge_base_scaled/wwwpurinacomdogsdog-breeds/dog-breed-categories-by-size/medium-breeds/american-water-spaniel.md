# American Water Spaniel

The **American Water Spaniel** is a rare, medium-sized U.S.-bred hunting and companion dog, known for its intelligence, high energy, and eagerness to please. Standing **15–18 inches tall** and weighing **25–45 lbs**, this breed features a **wavy-to-curly coat** in solid liver, brown, or dark chocolate with possible white markings. Renowned for its **swimming prowess** (using its tail as a rudder), it excels in **field trials, hunting, agility, obedience, and therapy work**. Requiring **daily exercise** and **regular grooming** (twice-weekly brushing), this active breed thrives in active households. With only ~3,000 registered dogs, it’s a lesser-known yet versatile sporting breed.
