# Afghan Hound

The **Afghan Hound** is a **medium-sized, high-energy sighthound** (25–27" tall, 50–60 lbs) with a **long, low-shedding coat** and aristocratic temperament. Bred for speed and agility, its **protruding hip joints** and large paw pads enable swift, obstacle-avoiding movement, but its **instinctual prey drive** demands **secure, off-leash-free environments**. Known for independence and loyalty to family, it thrives on **daily exercise** (agility, racing) and **high-protein nutrition** to sustain its athletic build. Health considerations include **anesthesia sensitivity** (low body fat), **bloat risk** (deep chest), **ear infections** (pendulous ears), and hereditary issues (hip/eye/thyroid). Requiring **regular grooming** (daily brushing, bathing) to prevent tangles, this ancient breed (dating back millennia) originated in Afghanistan/India/Pakistan as elite hunting companions for mountain kingdoms.
