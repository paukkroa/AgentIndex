# Cardigan Welsh Corgi

The **Cardigan Welsh Corgi** is a medium-sized, even-tempered breed with a short double coat, standing **10.5–12.5 inches tall** and weighing **25–38 lbs**. Known for its **loyalty, intelligence, and adaptability**, this historic herding breed (dating back to **1200 B.C.**) excels in agility, obedience, and tracking. Originally bred as a farm dog in Wales, it retains a **medium-energy** nature, thriving in both urban and rural settings with **daily exercise**. Its coat—available in **red, sable, brindle, or merle patterns**—requires **regular brushing** to maintain. Historically, it was recognized as distinct from the Pembroke Welsh Corgi only in **1934**. Recommended for active owners, it pairs well with **joint-supportive diets** and suits multi-pet households.
