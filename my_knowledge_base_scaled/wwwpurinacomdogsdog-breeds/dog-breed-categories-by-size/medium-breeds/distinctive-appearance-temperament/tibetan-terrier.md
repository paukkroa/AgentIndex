# Tibetan Terrier

The **Tibetan Terrier** is a medium-sized, long-coated companion breed with a rich history spanning over 2,000 years, originally bred in Tibet’s harsh climate for resilience. Standing **14–17 inches tall** and weighing **18–30 lbs**, this intelligent, sensitive, and mischievous breed thrives on **medium energy**, excelling in activities like agility, obedience, and rally. Despite its name, it is not a true terrier but shares a similar size. Known for its **loyal, affectionate nature**, it requires **positive training, regular exercise, and weekly grooming** due to its dense coat. Dietary recommendations include **Pro Plan’s sensitive skin formulas** (salmon/rice or turkey/oatmeal) for skin/stomach health, and **puppy-specific blends** for growing dogs. Ideal for owners seeking a **low-shedding, medium-energy, long-haired** breed with a vocal yet gentle temperament.
