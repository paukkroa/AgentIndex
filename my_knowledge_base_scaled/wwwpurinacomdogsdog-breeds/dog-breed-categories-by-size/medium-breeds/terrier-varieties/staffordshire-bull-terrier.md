# Staffordshire Bull Terrier

The **Staffordshire Bull Terrier** is a medium-sized, short-coated breed (14–16" tall, 24–38 lbs) with a **sweet, intelligent, and courageous temperament**, originally developed in 19th-century England from Bulldog and Mastiff lines. Known for its **affectionate nature—especially with children**—this breed thrives on **daily exercise** (ideal for agility, obedience, or conformation) and requires **firm yet gentle training** due to its strength and determination. Its **low-maintenance coat** only needs weekly brushing, while its powerful jaws benefit from **durable chew toys**. Historically linked to working breeds, the Staffordshire Bull Terrier balances agility with loyalty, making it a **high-energy yet loyal companion** for active owners.
