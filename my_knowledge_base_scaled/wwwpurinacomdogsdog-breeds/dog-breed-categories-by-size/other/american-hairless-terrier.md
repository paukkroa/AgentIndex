# American Hairless Terrier

The **American Hairless Terrier** is a small, energetic breed (12–28 lbs) native to the U.S., descended from Rat Terriers and Smooth Fox Terriers. Known for its **hairless or short-coated** varieties (with potential sunburn risk), it boasts a **tenacious, playful temperament** and **medium energy**, making it ideal for active owners. Low-allergenic, these dogs require **high-protein diets** (small-breed formulas) to support metabolism, with **scheduled feeding** to prevent obesity. Grooming focuses on **skin care** (moisturizing for hairless types) and **sun protection**, while their **high prey drive** demands mental stimulation and secure environments. Descended from pest-control Rat Terriers, they retain a **feisty, curious nature** and thrive with consistent training and exercise.
