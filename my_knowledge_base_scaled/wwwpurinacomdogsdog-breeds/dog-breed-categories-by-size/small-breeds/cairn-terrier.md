# Cairn Terrier

The **Cairn Terrier** is a small, hardy breed originally bred in Scotland to flush out burrowing animals like foxes, rats, and otters from rock cairns (piles of stones). Known for its **compact build, broad head, and distinctive shaggy coat** (medium-long, requiring **weekly brushing, occasional trimming/stripping, and monthly bathing**), this tenacious terrier exhibits **medium energy**, making it well-suited for **hunting, agility, obedience, and earthdog trials**. With a **height of 9.5–10 inches** and **weight of 13–14 lbs**, it thrives on mental and physical stimulation due to its **instinctive digging and chasing behaviors**. Historically, Cairns were named for their role in alerting farmers by barking from rock dens; the breed gained fame through **Toto from *The Wizard of Oz***. Ideal for active owners, this breed’s **low-shedding coat** and adaptable nature also make it a great fit for small-space living.
