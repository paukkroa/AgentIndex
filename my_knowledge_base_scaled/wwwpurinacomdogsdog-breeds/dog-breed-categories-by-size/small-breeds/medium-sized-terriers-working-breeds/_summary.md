This section explores **Medium-Sized Terriers & Working Breeds**, highlighting two distinct yet hardworking terrier varieties: the **Bedlington Terrier**, a compact, curly-coated breed known for its moderate energy and elegant appearance (15–16" tall, 17–23 lbs), and the **Dandie Dinmont Terrier**, a low-to-the-ground companion with a signature pepper-and-mustard coat (8–11" tall, 18–24 lbs). Both breeds embody classic terrier traits—alertness, tenacity, and adaptability—while catering to owners seeking a balance between manageable size and spirited personality. Ideal for active yet moderate households, these breeds offer unique charm, historical significance, and a blend of working instincts with companionable affection.
