# Bedlington Terrier

The **Bedlington Terrier** is a small, medium-coated breed originating from 19th-century England, originally bred for vermin control. Known for its distinctive curly, woolly coat and elegant, athletic build, this breed excels in speed and endurance. Standing **15–16 inches tall** and weighing **17–23 lbs**, it comes in colors like blue, sandy, liver, or tan variations. With a **medium energy level**, the Bedlington thrives on daily exercise and enjoys activities like agility, obedience, and conformation shows. Its gentle, affectionate temperament makes it an ideal family pet, though its coat requires regular grooming. Historically favored by figures like the Rockefeller family, this breed balances grace, intelligence, and moderate activity, making it a versatile companion.
