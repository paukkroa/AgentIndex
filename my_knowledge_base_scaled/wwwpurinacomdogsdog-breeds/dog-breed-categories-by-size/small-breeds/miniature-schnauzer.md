# Miniature Schnauzer

The **Miniature Schnauzer** is a small, spirited breed (12–14" tall, 14–17 lbs) descended from the Standard Schnauzer, originally bred for vermin control on farms. Known for its **stocky, muscular build**, **distinctive wiry coat** (black, black-and-silver, or salt-and-pepper), and **alert, intelligent expression** (with bushy eyebrows and whiskered muzzle), this breed excels as a **watchdog and companion**. With **medium energy**, it thrives in activities like agility, obedience, and hunting. Its **nonshedding, low-maintenance coat** requires regular grooming to prevent matting. Celebrated by notable figures like Mary Tyler Moore and Bruce Lee, the Miniature Schnauzer combines **loyalty, trainability, and affection**, making it a versatile yet manageable pet for active households.
