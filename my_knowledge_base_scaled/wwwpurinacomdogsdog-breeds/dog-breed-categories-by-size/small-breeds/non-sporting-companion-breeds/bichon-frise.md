# Bichon Frise

The **Bichon Frise** is a small, sturdy, and affectionate **Non-Sporting** breed known for its **gentle temperament, intelligence, and playful energy**. Standing **9.5–11.5 inches tall** and weighing **12–18 lbs**, this breed thrives as a **family companion**, excelling in activities like **agility, obedience, and rally training**. Its **medium-length, low-shedding white coat** (with possible buff/cream shading) requires **regular grooming every 4–6 weeks** to prevent mats. Recognized by the **AKC in 1973**, the Bichon’s history spans royal courts (Spain, Italy, France) to street performances post-Revolution. With a **lifespan of 14–15 years**, this breed is generally healthy but prone to **allergies, patellar luxation, and dental issues**. Ideal for allergy sufferers, Bichons benefit from **small-breed dog food** and **puppy-specific nutrition** for proper growth.
