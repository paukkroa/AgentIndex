# Australian Terrier

The **Australian Terrier** is a small, sturdy, and spirited breed originally bred in Australia for vermin control and livestock protection. Standing **10–11 inches (males) / 9–10 inches (females)** tall and weighing **11–16 lbs**, this breed features a **short, waterproof, low-shedding coat** in colors like **blue-and-tan, sandy, or solid red**. Known for its **medium energy**, alert personality, and **affectionate yet self-assured** nature, the Australian Terrier thrives in urban, suburban, or rural settings. Excels in **agility, obedience, and conformation** competitions. Recognized as Australia’s first breed shown in dog shows, it’s an ideal **family companion** with minimal grooming needs.
