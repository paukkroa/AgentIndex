# Smooth Fox Terrier

The **Smooth Fox Terrier** is a small, medium-energy breed (16–18 lbs) with a dense, flat, hard coat in white, black/tan, or combinations, predominantly white. Originating in 17th-century England as foxhunting companions—bred from Old English Terriers, Bull Terriers, Greyhounds, and Beagles—they were designed to fit in saddlebags. Known for their playful, outgoing, and intelligent yet occasionally stubborn personalities, they thrive on learning but require consistent training. Their care includes high-quality small-breed kibble (e.g., Purina Pro Plan Small Breed), portion-controlled feeding (10% treats max), and vet-approved supplements (e.g., joint support or probiotics). While generally healthy, routine veterinary care (vaccinations, checkups) is essential. Historically called the "gentleman of the terrier world," they balance energy with elegance, making them lively yet manageable companions.
