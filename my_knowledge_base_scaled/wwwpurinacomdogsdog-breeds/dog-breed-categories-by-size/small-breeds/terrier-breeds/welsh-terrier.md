# Welsh Terrier

The **Welsh Terrier** is a small, high-energy companion and working terrier with a distinctive wiry coat in **black-and-tan or grizzle-and-tan**, originally bred for hunting badgers, foxes, and otters in Wales. Males stand **15–15.5 inches** tall, while females reach **14 inches**, weighing **18–20 lbs**. Known for their **spunky, intelligent, and outgoing personality**, they thrive in active households and excel in **agility, conformation, hunting, and earthdog trials**. Their **bearded muzzle and bushy eyebrows** enhance their expressive look. Grooming requires **regular coat plucking** (2–3x/year) and **weekly facial hair brushing** to maintain their wiry texture. Historically linked to figures like **Caroline Kennedy**, this breed balances playfulness with attentiveness, making them ideal **watchdogs and family pets**.
