# Pug

The **Pug** is a small, sturdy, and affectionate breed (10–13" tall, 14–18 lbs) known for its playful, mischievous, and loving temperament, embodying the Latin motto *"multum in parvo"* ("a lot in a little"). With a short, low-maintenance coat and adaptable nature, Pugs thrive in urban or rural settings, making them ideal family pets. While they enjoy moderate exercise like agility and obedience training, their sensitivity requires gentle handling. Key health concerns include breathing difficulties (due to their flat face), eye issues (e.g., corneal ulcers), and obesity risks from their food-loving nature. Lifespan averages **13–15 years**. Their classic fawn-and-black mask color and minimal grooming needs (weekly brushing) complement their easygoing yet lively personality. Historically rooted in ancient China (2,000+ years), Pugs were prized by emperors before evolving into beloved companions worldwide.
