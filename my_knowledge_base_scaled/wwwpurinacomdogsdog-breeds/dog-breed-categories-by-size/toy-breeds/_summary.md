The **Toy Breeds** section explores a diverse array of miniature yet distinctive canine companions, each celebrated for their unique physical traits, personalities, and historical significance. From the expressive, human-like faces of the **Brussels Griffon** to the bold confidence of the **Chihuahua**, these breeds range from the luxurious-coated **Yorkshire Terrier** to the low-shedding elegance of the **Maltese** and **Chinese Crested**. Compact yet spirited, these toy breeds—including the **Toy Fox Terrier** and **English Toy Spaniel**—offer a blend of charm, adaptability, and low-maintenance care, making them ideal for various lifestyles.
