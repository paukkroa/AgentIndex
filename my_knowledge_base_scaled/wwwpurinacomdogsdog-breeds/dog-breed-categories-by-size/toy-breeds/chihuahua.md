# Chihuahua

The **Chihuahua** is a diminutive yet bold toy breed, renowned for its Napoleon-like confidence and sassy yet affectionate temperament. Standing at **5–8 inches tall** and weighing up to **6 pounds**, this breed thrives as a lapdog, ideal for city living due to its **medium energy** and minimal exercise needs. Chihuahuas excel in **conformation, obedience, and rally obedience** competitions but require **consistent training** to curb their dominant tendencies. Their **short or long coats** are medium shedders, with seasonal heavy shedding. Known for their **lifespan of 14–16 years**, Chihuahuas are adaptable companions, though prone to genetic health issues like **heart disease, epilepsy, and patellar luxation**. AKC-approved in **any color**, they pair well with **small-breed-specific dog foods** (e.g., Purina Pro Plan Toy Breed) and **puppy formulas rich in DHA** for optimal growth.
