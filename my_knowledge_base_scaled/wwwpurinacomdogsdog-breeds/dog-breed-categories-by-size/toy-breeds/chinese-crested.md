# Chinese Crested

The **Chinese Crested** is a slender, fine-boned toy breed with origins linked to African hairless dogs, historically accompanying Chinese sailors as companions. Known for its elegant and graceful appearance, this medium-energy breed comes in two distinct coat varieties: **hairless** (minimal shedding) and **powderpuff** (short/medium coat). Males reach **11–13 inches** (7–12 lbs) and females **9–12 inches** (5–11 lbs). Ideal for agility, obedience, and rally events, the Chinese Crested thrives as a playful, affectionate indoor companion. Notable for their low-shedding traits and hypoallergenic appeal, they’ve appeared in films (*"102 Dalmatians," "How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days"*) and TV (*"Ugly Betty"*). Recommended products include hypoallergenic diets (e.g., **Pro Plan Sensitive Skin**) and weight-management options.
