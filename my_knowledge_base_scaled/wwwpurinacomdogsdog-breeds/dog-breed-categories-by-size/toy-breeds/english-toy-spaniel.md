# English Toy Spaniel

The **English Toy Spaniel** is a small, compact companion breed (10+ inches tall, 8–14 lbs) known for its gentle, playful, and affectionate temperament, making it ideal for families and urban living. With a **medium-length coat** in four distinct color varieties—Blenheim (red & white), Ruby (solid red), Prince Charles (white with black/tan), or King Charles (black & tan)—grooming is minimal, requiring only **biweekly brushing**. Historically favored by royalty (including Mary Queen of Scots), this low-energy breed excels in **conformation, obedience, and rally obedience** competitions. Its adaptable nature and low-shedding coat suit apartment dwellers, while its historical charm and adaptability make it a beloved indoor pet.
