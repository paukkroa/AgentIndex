# Maltese

The **Maltese** is a historic, elegant **toy breed** (7–9" tall, 4–6 lbs) with a **long, silky white coat** (often with dark eyes/nose) and **high energy**, excelling in **conformation, obedience, and agility**. Known for its **gentle yet bold temperament**, this low-shedding companion thrives on affection, training (reward-based), and daily grooming to prevent tangles. While generally healthy with a **lifespan of 12–15 years**, they require **regular dental care** and may face issues like patellar luxation or heart problems. Originating in **ancient Malta** (pre-dating the Bible), the breed was revered by Greeks, Romans, and Egyptians, later refined by Chinese breeders. Ideal for **small-breed-specific food** (e.g., dry kibble for dental health) and **active households**, the Maltese embodies a mix of **companionship, watchdog instincts, and historical significance**.
