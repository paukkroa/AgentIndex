# Toy Fox Terrier

The **Toy Fox Terrier** is a compact, high-energy companion breed (8.5–11.5" tall, 3.5–7 lbs) with a sleek, short coat in classic tri-color patterns (white/black/tan, chocolate/tan, etc.). Known for its bold, animated personality, this breed blends the Fox Terrier’s alertness with the sociable traits of toy breeds like the Miniature Pinscher and Chihuahua, originating in the 1930s as a calmer alternative. Ideal for families, it excels in watchdog duties due to its fearless, loyal nature while requiring minimal grooming (weekly brushing) and low exercise demands. Popular activities include conformation, obedience, and agility. Recommended for indoor living, it pairs well with high-protein diets and thrives in medium-energy dog communities.
