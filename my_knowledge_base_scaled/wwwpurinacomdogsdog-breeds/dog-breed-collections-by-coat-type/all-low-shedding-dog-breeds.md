# All Low-Shedding Dog Breeds

This page provides a curated list of **low-shedding dog breeds**, ideal for allergy sufferers or those seeking minimal fur cleanup. Low-shedding dogs typically possess **single-layer coats** (e.g., Basenji, Bichon Frise) or **dense, curly/woolly undercoats** (e.g., Bergamasco, Bearded Collie), which trap loose hair and reduce visible shedding. While these breeds shed less than double-coated breeds (e.g., Huskies), they often require **regular grooming** (brushing, trimming, or professional grooming) to prevent matting and maintain coat health. The page highlights **15 breeds**, including terriers (Affenpinscher, Bedlington), spaniels (American Water Spaniel), and herding dogs (Bouvier des Flandres), each with unique coat textures (short, wiry, or hypoallergenic). Note: "Low-shedding" isn’t synonymous with "non-shedding"—all dogs shed, but these breeds minimize visible hair on furniture/clothing.
