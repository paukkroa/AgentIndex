# All Medium-Haired Dog Breeds

This page highlights **medium-haired dog breeds**, offering a balanced alternative to short- or long-haired varieties. Medium coats are typically **softer, fluffier, and lower-maintenance** than coarse short-haired breeds, while requiring less upkeep than long-haired breeds. The page features **12 breeds**, including active working dogs (e.g., **Border Collie, Australian Shepherd**), sturdy terriers (e.g., **Airedale Terrier, Bedlington Terrier**), and companion breeds (e.g., **Bichon Frise, American Eskimo Dog**). Ideal for owners seeking **versatile, adaptable pets** with a **moderate grooming commitment**, the breeds listed vary in temperament, energy levels, and historical roles—from herding and hunting to companionship.
