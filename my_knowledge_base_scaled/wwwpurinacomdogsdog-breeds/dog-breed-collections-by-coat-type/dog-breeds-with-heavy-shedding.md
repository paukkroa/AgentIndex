# Dog Breeds with Heavy Shedding

This page explores **dog breeds known for heavy shedding**, debunking the myth that coat length directly correlates with shedding. It provides a curated list of **20 high-shedding breeds**, including iconic working dogs (e.g., **Akita, Alaskan Malamute**), herding breeds (e.g., **Belgian Malinois, Belgian Sheepdog**), and hounds (e.g., **Basset Hound, Beagle**). Each breed’s shedding tendencies stem from **genetic factors, double-layered coats, or seasonal molting**, making regular grooming and vacuuming essential. Ideal for pet owners who prioritize **honesty about maintenance** or seek breeds with **distinctive, voluminous coats**, this resource highlights practical considerations for managing fur in homes.
