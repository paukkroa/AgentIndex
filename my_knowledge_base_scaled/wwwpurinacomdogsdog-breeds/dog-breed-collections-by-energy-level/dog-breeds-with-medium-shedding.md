# Dog Breeds with Medium Shedding

This page curates **dog breeds with medium shedding**, ideal for owners who tolerate occasional vacuuming but prefer less fur than high-shedding breeds. The list includes **15 diverse breeds**—such as the **Affenpinscher, Afghan Hound, and Australian Shepherd**—each with distinct temperaments, sizes, and energy levels. Medium-shedding dogs typically require **regular brushing (2–3x/week)** and **weekly grooming** to manage loose fur, though they shed less than double-coated breeds like Huskies or Shetland Sheepdogs. The page emphasizes practicality for allergy-conscious pet owners while acknowledging that shedding varies by breed, coat type (single/double), and individual health. Filtering options (if available) help narrow choices based on activity level, size, or working background (e.g., herding, hunting).
