# All Low-Energy Dog Breeds

This page highlights **low-energy dog breeds** ideal for owners seeking relaxed, low-maintenance companions, particularly suited for urban living (e.g., apartments) or individuals with limited mobility or time for vigorous exercise. It contrasts with high-energy breeds, emphasizing breeds like the **Affenpinscher, Basset Hound, or Bulldog**, known for their calm demeanor, minimal exercise needs, and adaptability to indoor lifestyles. The page includes a filterable list of **10 breeds**, each with distinct traits—such as the **Great Pyrenees’** guarding instincts or the **Bloodhound’s** scent-tracking tendencies—while prioritizing breeds that thrive on short walks, cuddles, and minimal stimulation. Perfect for first-time owners or those prioritizing tranquility over activity.
