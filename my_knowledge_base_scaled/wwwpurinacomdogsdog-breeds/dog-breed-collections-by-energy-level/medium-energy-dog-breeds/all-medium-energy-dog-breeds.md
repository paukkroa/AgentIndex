# All Medium-Energy Dog Breeds

This page provides a curated list of **medium-energy dog breeds**, ideal for owners seeking a balance between active engagement and relaxed companionship. Medium-energy breeds typically require **moderate daily exercise** (30–60 minutes) and mental stimulation, making them suitable for individuals or households with a **steady routine** but not extreme demands. The breeds listed—ranging from **hunting companions (e.g., Bloodhound, Bluetick Coonhound)** to **working dogs (e.g., Boerboel, Cane Corso)** and **affectionate lapdogs (e.g., Cavalier King Charles Spaniel, Brussels Griffon)**—offer diverse temperaments, sizes, and care needs. Key considerations include **trainability, grooming requirements, and adaptability to urban/suburban living**, ensuring compatibility with lifestyle preferences. The page emphasizes **matching energy levels** to avoid mismatches that could lead to behavioral or health issues, while also highlighting breeds that thrive on **structured activity** without requiring marathon workouts.
