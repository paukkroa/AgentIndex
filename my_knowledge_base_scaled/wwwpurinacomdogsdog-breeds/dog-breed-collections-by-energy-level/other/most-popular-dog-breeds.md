# Most Popular Dog Breeds

This page highlights the **10 most popular dog breeds in America**, offering a diverse selection of medium to large breeds, including energetic retrievers like the **Golden Retriever** and **Labrador Retriever**, loyal guardians such as the **German Shepherd** and **Rottweiler**, and compact companions like the **Beagle** and **Yorkshire Terrier**. Each breed is linked to detailed profiles covering temperament, care requirements, and suitability for different lifestyles. The page also includes a filterable list for easy navigation, allowing users to explore breeds based on size preferences (e.g., small breeds) or specific traits. Ideal for potential pet owners seeking well-researched, widely-loved options.
