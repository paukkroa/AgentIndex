# All Best Companion Dog Breeds

This page provides an overview of the **top companion dog breeds**, ideal for various lifestyles, from active households to seniors or families with children. It highlights breeds known for loyalty, adaptability, and affection, including **Border Collies** (high-energy, trainable), **Golden Retrievers** (friendly, family-oriented), **Bichon Frises** (gentle, hypoallergenic), and **Bulldogs** (calm, low-energy). The page features a filterable list of **10 breeds**, each with detailed breed profiles linking to in-depth information on temperament, care, and suitability. Perfect for users seeking a tailored match based on activity level, size, or household dynamics.
