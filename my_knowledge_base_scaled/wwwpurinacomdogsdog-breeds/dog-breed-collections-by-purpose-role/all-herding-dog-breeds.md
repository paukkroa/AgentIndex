# All Herding Dog Breeds

Herding dog breeds, including iconic working dogs like **Border Collies** and versatile **Australian Shepherds**, excel in livestock management but adapt to diverse roles with proper training and exercise. This category spans **large breeds** (e.g., **Beauceron, Briard**) to compact yet agile **small herding dogs** like the **Corgi**, showcasing a range of temperaments, coat types, and working styles. Ideal for farmers, ranchers, or active owners seeking intelligent, energetic companions, these breeds thrive on mental stimulation and physical activity, making them adaptable to herding, protection, or even family life with the right environment.
