# All Non-Sporting Dog Breeds

The **All Non-Sporting Dog Breeds** page highlights breeds tailored for owners seeking low-to-moderate energy companions rather than high-performance working or hunting partners. Non-sporting dogs, while still requiring regular exercise (e.g., walks or fetch), are generally more adaptable to apartment living or relaxed lifestyles. This collection features **12 distinct breeds**, including the **American Eskimo Dog** (known for intelligence and alertness), **Bichon Frise** (hypoallergenic and affectionate), **Boston Terrier** (compact and social), **Bulldog** (calm and low-energy), and **Chinese Shar-Pei** (distinctive wrinkled coat and independent temperament). Other breeds like the **Dalmatian** (iconic spotted coat) and **French Bulldog** (companionable and low-maintenance) cater to diverse preferences, from lap dogs to slightly larger, sturdy companions. The page emphasizes **filtering options** to help users quickly identify breeds matching size, temperament, or grooming needs, making it ideal for first-time or experienced dog owners prioritizing temperament over athleticism.
