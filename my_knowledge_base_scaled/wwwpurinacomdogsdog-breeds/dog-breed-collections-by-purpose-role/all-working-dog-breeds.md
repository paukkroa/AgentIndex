# All Working Dog Breeds

This page highlights **working dog breeds**, historically bred for specialized tasks such as herding, guarding livestock, sled pulling, hunting, and protection. Primarily large breeds, these dogs thrive with purpose-driven roles, even when repurposed for non-traditional activities. The list includes **15 breeds** like the Akita, Alaskan Malamute, and Bullmastiff, each with distinct traits suited for labor, vigilance, or companionship. Ideal for active owners seeking a task-oriented, loyal, and robust canine partner.
