# Best Indoor Dogs

This guide highlights **indoor-friendly dog breeds** ideal for urban or small-space living, emphasizing adaptability, low-energy needs, and companionship. It covers **13 breeds**—ranging from small lap dogs (e.g., **Bichon Frise**) to sturdy yet low-activity breeds (e.g., **Basenji, Afghan Hound**)—that thrive on indoor play, cuddles, and minimal outdoor exercise. Key traits include **moderate grooming requirements**, **moderate to low exercise demands**, and **affectionate, people-oriented temperaments**. The page provides breed-specific insights, helping pet owners select a **calm, social, and manageable** companion for apartment or house living without a yard.
