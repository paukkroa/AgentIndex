# All Hypoallergenic Dog Breeds

This page provides a curated list of **hypoallergenic dog breeds** ideal for individuals with allergies, minimizing reactions to dander, saliva, or fur. Hypoallergenic dogs typically shed minimally or produce less allergenic proteins (e.g., **Basenji**, **Bichon Frise**, **Giant Schnauzer**, **Havanese**, **Miniature Schnauzer**, **Poodle**, and **Portuguese Water Dog**), making them safer companions. The breeds listed are known for their low-shedding coats (often curly or dense) and reduced allergen production, though no dog is 100% hypoallergenic. Each breed’s page offers detailed insights into temperament, grooming needs, and suitability for allergy sufferers, ensuring informed decision-making for prospective owners.
