# Best Dog Breeds for People with Dander Allergies

This guide highlights **hypoallergenic dog breeds** ideal for individuals with **dander allergies**, minimizing allergic reactions through minimal shedding and reduced allergen production. Key breeds—such as the **Basenji, Chinese Crested, Havanese, Poodle, Portuguese Water Dog, and Standard Schnauzer**—are selected for their **low-shedding coats** (often curly or hair-like rather than fur) and **hypoallergenic traits**, which significantly reduce airborne allergens like **canine dander (Can f 1, Can f 2, and Can f 4 proteins)**. The page emphasizes **breed-specific traits** (e.g., grooming needs, temperament) and provides **direct links** to detailed breed profiles, aiding informed decision-making for allergy sufferers seeking a furry companion.
