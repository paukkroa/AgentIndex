# Collie

The **Collie** is a large, intelligent herding breed originating from Scotland and England, known for its **graceful, swift, and protective** nature. With **medium energy**, it excels in **herding, tracking, service work, agility, and obedience**, making it a versatile working dog. Two coat varieties exist: the **long-haired Rough-Coated Collie** and the **short-haired Smooth-Coated Collie**, both requiring **moderate grooming**. Males stand **24–26 inches** (60–75 lbs) while females reach **22–24 inches** (50–65 lbs). Common colors include **sable & white, tricolor, blue merle, or solid white**. Historically significant—featured in Queen Victoria’s court and popularized by *Lassie*—Collies are **affectionate with children**, making them ideal family pets. Recommended for active households, they thrive in structured activities but adapt well to moderate exercise routines.
