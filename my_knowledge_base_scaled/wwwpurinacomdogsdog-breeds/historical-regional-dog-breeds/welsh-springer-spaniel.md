# Welsh Springer Spaniel

The **Welsh Springer Spaniel** is a medium-sized, high-energy hunting breed with a muscular build, strong olfactory sense, and webbed feet for agility in water. Standing **17–19 inches tall** and weighing **35–55 lbs**, this ancient breed (dating back to **7000 B.C.**) is known for its **"springing" action** when flushing game, earning its name. With a **red-and-white medium-length coat**, it requires **weekly brushing** to maintain its feathered texture. Affectionate, loyal, and sensitive ("Velcro dog"), it thrives on **daily vigorous exercise** and excels in **hunting, agility, obedience, and field trials**. Ideal for active owners, it pairs well with high-protein diets (e.g., salmon/rice or turkey-based formulas) to support its athletic lifestyle.
