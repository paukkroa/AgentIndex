This section explores the diverse roles and characteristics of large dog breeds, encompassing their specialized functions in protection, hunting, working, herding, and companionship. Working and Utility Dogs cover specialized roles like search-and-rescue or service work, while Herding and Farm Dogs emphasize breeds like Border Collies and Australian Shepherds. Unique breeds like the Borzoi and Wirehaired Pointing Griffon are detailed for their distinct traits, including elegance, energy levels, and hunting prowess. The section concludes with an overview of other large breeds, summarizing their temperaments and roles to provide a comprehensive understanding of their capabilities and suitability for different lifestyles.
