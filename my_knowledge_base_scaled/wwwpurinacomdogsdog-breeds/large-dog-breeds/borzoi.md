# Borzoi

The **Borzoi** is a majestic **Giant sighthound** originating from Tsarist Russia, renowned for its **speed, agility, and courage** in hunting wolves, foxes, and hares. Standing **26–28+ inches tall** and weighing **65–105 lbs**, this **high-energy** breed boasts a **long, lustrous coat** in any color. While historically bred for endurance, modern Borzois thrive as **affectionate family companions**, requiring **daily vigorous exercise** (e.g., field trials, running) and **regular grooming** to maintain their coat. Notable for their elegance, they’ve appeared in films like *Lady and the Tramp* and *102 Dalmatians*. Ideal for active owners, they pair well with similar breeds like **Afghans or Greyhounds**.
