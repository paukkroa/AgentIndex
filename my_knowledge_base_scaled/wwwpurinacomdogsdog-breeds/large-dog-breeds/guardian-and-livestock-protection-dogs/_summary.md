The **Guardian and Livestock Protection Dogs** section explores powerful, historically bred canines designed for protection, herding, and defense. Featuring breeds like the **Anatolian Shepherd** (a 6,000-year-old Turkish guardian), **Boerboel** (South Africa’s formidable guard dog), **Bullmastiff** (a low-energy, family-oriented giant), and **Cane Corso** (a muscular Italian mastiff), this section highlights their distinct roles—from livestock defense to home security—while covering physical traits, temperaments, and care essentials for responsible ownership.
