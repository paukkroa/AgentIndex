# Anatolian Shepherd Dog

The **Anatolian Shepherd Dog**, an ancient Turkish breed dating back **6,000 years**, is a **large to giant** working dog (males: **20–27" tall, 110–150 lbs**; females: **80–120 lbs**) bred for **livestock protection** and shepherd companionship. Known for its **medium-length coat** (short or rough varieties) in colors like **fawn with black mask, pinto, white, or brindle**, this breed exhibits **medium energy**, excelling in **conformation and herding activities**. Highly **loyal and possessive** of its family, it remains **suspicious of strangers**, requiring early socialization. The breed **matures slowly**, reaching full adulthood around **4 years old**, and thrives with **moderate exercise** and minimal grooming needs. Recommended products include **Pro Plan puppy formulas** for proper nutrition.
