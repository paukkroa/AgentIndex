# Boerboel

The **Boerboel** is a large, powerful working breed originating from 17th-century South Africa, historically bred as a versatile farm dog for protection and livestock management. Standing **22–27 inches tall** and weighing **110–200 lbs**, this muscular breed features a **smooth, low-maintenance coat** in colors like mahogany, fawn, brindle, or piebald. Known for its **medium energy, intelligence, and calm confidence**, the Boerboel excels in **protection work, obedience, agility, and therapy roles**, while also serving as a loyal family companion. Requiring **firm, structured training** due to its dominant nature, this breed thrives on **loyalty, protective instincts, and minimal grooming needs**. Historically called the "farm dog," it remains a formidable yet affectionate guardian.
