# Bergamasco

The **Bergamasco** is an ancient Alpine breed, dating back over 2,000 years, originally bred for herding and livestock guarding. Standing **22–23.5 inches tall** and weighing **57–84 lbs**, this large, medium-energy dog thrives as a **loyal family companion**, valuing human interaction and disliking prolonged solitude. Its **hypoallergenic, corded coat**—comprising undercoat, "goat hair," and outer hair—forms distinctive mats (felt-like cords) that fully develop by age 6, requiring minimal grooming beyond occasional bathing. Unique features include **extra-long eyelashes** (protecting against snowblindness) and a gentle, patient temperament. Ideal for **conformation, herding, guarding, and agility**, the Bergamasco also benefits from regular nail trims and dental care.
