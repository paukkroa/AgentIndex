# Bluetick Coonhound

The **Bluetick Coonhound** is a large, athletic hound breed known for its distinctive **dark blue, mottled coat with black spots and tan markings**, earning its name from its unique coloration. Males stand **22–27 inches** (55–80 lbs) and females **21–25 inches** (45–65 lbs), with a short, low-maintenance coat requiring minimal grooming. Renowned for **exceptional tracking and treeing skills**, this breed excels in hunting, field trials, and conformation shows. Highly intelligent, loyal, and affectionate, Blueticks bond well with families and other pets. Born mostly white with dark patches, their ticking fully develops within weeks. As **Tennessee’s state dog** and the University of Tennessee’s mascot, this medium-energy breed thrives in active environments but remains a gentle companion.
