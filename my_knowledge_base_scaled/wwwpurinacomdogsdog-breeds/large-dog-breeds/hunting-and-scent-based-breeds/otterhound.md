# Otterhound

The **Otterhound** is a rare, large English scenthound (under 1,000 worldwide) bred for land-and-water otter hunting, featuring a **24–27" tall, 80–115 lb** robust build, medium-length shaggy coat, and webbed feet. Known for its **deep bark, medium energy**, and **good-natured temperament**, this breed excels in **hunting, tracking, and conformation shows**. Requires **weekly grooming** to maintain its dense, coarse coat and prevent matting. Ideal for active families seeking a **loyal, vocal, and medium-shedding** companion.
