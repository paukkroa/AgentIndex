# Large Dog Breeds

This page provides an overview of **large dog breeds**, highlighting their diverse temperaments—ranging from highly active and agile (e.g., **Saluki**, **Vizsla**) to more laid-back companions (e.g., **Siberian Husky**, **Weimaraner**). Ideal for owners seeking athletic partners (hiking, running) or cuddly, low-energy lapdogs, the list includes **eight breeds** with distinct traits: **Rottweilers** (protective/loyal), **Spinone Italianos** (versatile hunting/sporting), and **Treeing Walker Coonhounds** (energetic scent-trackers). Each breed links to detailed profiles covering temperament, exercise needs, grooming, and historical roles, ensuring informed selection based on lifestyle compatibility.
