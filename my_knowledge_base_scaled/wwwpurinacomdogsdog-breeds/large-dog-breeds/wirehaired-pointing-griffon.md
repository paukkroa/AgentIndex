# Wirehaired Pointing Griffon

The **Wirehaired Pointing Griffon** is a large, high-energy sporting breed (20–24" tall, 50–60 lbs) distinguished by its wiry, double-coated fur—typically brown/gray or chestnut/gray—that matures over **2–3 years**. Originating in **Holland** but refined in **France**, this intelligent, trainable, and affectionate hunter excels in **agility, obedience, hunting, and field trials**. Requiring **daily vigorous exercise** and social interaction, the Griffon thrives as a loyal family companion but needs consistent mental stimulation to prevent boredom. Its coarse coat demands regular grooming to maintain texture and health.
