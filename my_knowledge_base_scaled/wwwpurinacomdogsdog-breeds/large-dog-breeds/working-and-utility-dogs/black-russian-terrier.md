# Black Russian Terrier

The **Black Russian Terrier** is a modern, high-energy working breed developed post-WWII by the Soviet military’s **Red Star Kennel** to serve as a versatile guard and security dog. Standing **26–30 inches tall** and weighing **75–130 lbs**, this large breed features a **ruffled double coat** (black with occasional gray) designed for cold climates and protection. Bred from **17 parent breeds** (including Giant Schnauzer, Rottweiler, and Airedale Terrier), it combines intelligence, trainability, and a strong work ethic, excelling in **military/police work, search/rescue, agility, and Schutzhund**. Requiring **moderate exercise** and **extensive grooming** due to its dense coat, this breed thrives in structured roles but needs an active, engaged owner.
