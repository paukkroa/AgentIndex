# Bearded Collie

The **Bearded Collie**, one of Britain’s oldest herding breeds, is a medium-sized, agile dog (20–22" tall, 35–60 lbs) distinguished by its shaggy double coat—often black, blue, or fawn with white markings—and a distinctive "beard" under its chin. Descended from the Komondor and originally called the Highland Collie, this intelligent, high-energy breed thrives in activities like herding, agility, and obedience. Known for its playful, loyal temperament and ever-wagging tail, the Bearded Collie requires **regular grooming** to maintain its dense undercoat. Ideal for active owners, it excels in both working roles and as a devoted family companion.
