# Irish Wolfhound

The **Irish Wolfhound** is the world’s tallest dog breed, standing at **30–34 inches** and weighing **105+ lbs** (females) or **120+ lbs** (males), with a medium-length, dense coat in colors like grey, brindle, or black. Descended from the Greyhound lineage, this ancient breed excels in **endurance, agility, and hunting**, though it is **non-aggressive and affectionate**, making it a loyal family companion. Requiring **spacious exercise areas** and **regular brushing** for its coarse coat, the Irish Wolfhound thrives in **conformation, lure coursing, and obedience** activities. Historically owned by figures like **Anne Boleyn and Sting**, this breed balances **courage, trustworthiness, and moderate energy**, though its size demands attentive care.
