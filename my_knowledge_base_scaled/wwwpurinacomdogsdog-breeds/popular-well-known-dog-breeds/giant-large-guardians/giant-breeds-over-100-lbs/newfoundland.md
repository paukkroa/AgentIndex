# Newfoundland

The **Newfoundland** is a massive, gentle giant breed known for its intelligence, loyalty, and natural lifesaving instincts. Standing at **26–28 inches tall** and weighing **100–150 lbs**, this low-energy yet active breed thrives on human companionship, making it an ideal family pet. With a **short, thick coat** (moderate shedder) and webbed paws, Newfoundlands excel in water rescue, carting, and draft work. Their **patient, affectionate temperament** earned them the nickname "nanny dog," though early training and socialization are essential due to their size. Health concerns include **hip/elbow dysplasia, heart disease, and ear infections**, requiring responsible breeding and proper care. Historically bred as working dogs in Newfoundland and England, they remain beloved for their strength, resilience, and deep bond with families.
