The **Large Retrievers & Water Dogs** section explores iconic breeds renowned for their size, strength, and water-resistant traits, blending working ability with distinctive regional heritage. From the majestic **Bernese Mountain Dog**, a gentle giant from the Swiss Alps, to the rugged **Chesapeake Bay Retriever**, a hardy American water retriever built for endurance, this section highlights breeds that excel in both utility and companionship. Each breed’s unique coat, temperament, and historical purpose—whether as draft animals, hunting companions, or versatile working dogs—are examined in depth, offering insights into their care, training, and role in modern households.
