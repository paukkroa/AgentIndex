# Chesapeake Bay Retriever

The **Chesapeake Bay Retriever** is a large, athletic, and hardy American breed known for its waterproof double coat, designed for retrieving game in freezing Chesapeake Bay waters. Standing **21–26 inches tall** and weighing **55–80 lbs**, this breed exhibits **high energy**, **loyalty**, and **determination**, making it an exceptional hunting companion and versatile working dog. With a **short, dense coat** in shades of **brown or tan**, Chessies require **daily vigorous exercise** and **weekly brushing** to manage moderate shedding. While trainable, their independent streak demands consistent, patient handling. Recognized by the **AKC in 1884**, this breed traces its origins to **Newfoundland and Irish Water Spaniel** ancestry, bred for endurance in extreme conditions. Beyond hunting, Chessies excel in **obedience, tracking, Schutzhund, search-and-rescue, and therapy work**, with a **lifespan of 10–13 years**.
