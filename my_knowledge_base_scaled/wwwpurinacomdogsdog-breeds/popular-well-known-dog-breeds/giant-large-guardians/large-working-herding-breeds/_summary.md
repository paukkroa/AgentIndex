The **Large Working & Herding Breeds** section explores powerful, intelligent, and versatile canines bred for athleticism, protection, and herding tasks. Featuring breeds like the **Weimaraner**—a German Sporting dog known for speed and loyalty—alongside the **Beauceron**, a French herding giant with striking black-and-tan coats and boundless energy, and the **Giant Schnauzer**, a formidable guard dog with a robust build and low-shedding coat, this section highlights breeds distinguished by their size, working instincts, and distinctive temperaments. Each breed’s origins, physical traits, grooming needs, and activity requirements are detailed to inform prospective owners about their suitability for active lifestyles, family life, or specialized roles.
