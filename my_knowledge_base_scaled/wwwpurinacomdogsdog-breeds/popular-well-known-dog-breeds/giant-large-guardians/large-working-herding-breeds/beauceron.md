# Beauceron

The **Beauceron** is a **large/giant herding breed** originating in France (1500s) with a **short black-and-tan coat**, standing **24–27.5 inches tall** and weighing **65–110 lbs**. Known for its **high energy, intelligence, and natural guarding instincts**, this breed thrives with **experienced owners** due to its strong personality. Eager to learn, Beaucerons excel in training and require **daily vigorous exercise** to prevent boredom. Their **low-maintenance coat** needs minimal grooming, but their **loyal, gentle, and protective nature** makes them excellent companions. Historically, Beaucerons like **O’Cara Bleue de St. Sacrement** played critical roles in **search-and-rescue missions**, including post-9/11 disaster recovery, using specialized equipment to locate survivors.
