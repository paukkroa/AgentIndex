# Weimaraner

The **Weimaraner** is a large, athletic **Sporting breed** originating from Germany, renowned for its **high energy, intelligence, and distinctive short gray coat** (ranging from mouse-gray to silver-gray, often with a white chest spot). Males stand **25–27 inches** (70–90 lbs) and females **23–25 inches** (55–70 lbs), with a sleek, low-maintenance coat requiring only **weekly brushing**. Known as the *"Gray Ghost"* for their speed and endurance, Weimaraners thrive in **active lifestyles**, excelling in **agility, hunting, obedience, and tracking**. Their **high energy demands** necessitate rigorous exercise to prevent boredom or behavioral issues. Temperament-wise, they are **loyal, affectionate, and trainable**, making them excellent family pets—but their **deep chest** increases risks of **gastric torsion**, while their **pendulous ears** require regular cleaning.
