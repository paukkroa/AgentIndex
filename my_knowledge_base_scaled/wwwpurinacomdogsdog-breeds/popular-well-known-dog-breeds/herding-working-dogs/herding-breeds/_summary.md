The **Herding Breeds** section explores the diverse characteristics, origins, and care requirements of some of the world’s most intelligent and energetic working dogs. From the versatile **Australian Shepherd**, known for its Basque herding roots and adaptability, to the high-performance **Belgian Malinois**, celebrated for its speed and agility, this section highlights breeds like the **Belgian Sheepdog**, **Belgian Tervuren**, and the legendary **Border Collie**. Each breed is detailed with insights into their physical traits, energy levels, and specialized roles in herding, training, and companionship, offering a comprehensive guide for owners and enthusiasts.
