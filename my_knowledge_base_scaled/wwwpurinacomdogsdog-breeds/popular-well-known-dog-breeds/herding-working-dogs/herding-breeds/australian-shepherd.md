# Australian Shepherd

The **Australian Shepherd**, or "Aussie," is a medium-sized, highly intelligent, and energetic herding breed originally descended from Basque herding dogs in the Pyrenees Mountains. Standing **20–30 inches tall** (males) and weighing **35–70 lbs**, Aussies possess a **double-layer, waterproof coat** requiring **weekly brushing** (daily during shedding season) and come in colors like **black, blue merle, red, or red merle with tan points**. Known for their **herding instincts** (even on children or other pets) and **high energy**, they excel in **agility, obedience, dock diving, and guarding**, making them ideal for active owners. With a **lifespan of 12–15 years**, they are generally healthy but prone to **hip dysplasia, epilepsy, cataracts, and cancer**. Their **high activity needs** demand **daily exercise** (hiking, running, or canine sports) and a **high-protein diet** (e.g., Purina Pro Plan Sport formulas).
