# Border Collie

The **Border Collie** is a medium-sized, high-energy herding breed known for its intelligence, agility, and working drive. Standing **18–22 inches tall** and weighing **27–45 lbs**, they possess a **medium/long double coat** (rough or smooth) that sheds seasonally. Renowned for their **exceptional herding instincts**, Border Collies excel in **agility, obedience, rally, tracking, and flyball**, requiring **vigorous exercise** to prevent boredom. Their **affectionate yet task-oriented temperament** makes them ideal for active owners, though they may herd indiscriminately. Lifespan ranges **12–15 years**, with common health concerns including **hip dysplasia, eye anomalies, and epilepsy**. Nutritionally, they thrive on **high-protein, sport-formula diets** (e.g., Purina Pro Plan SPORT) or **puppy-specific foods** (e.g., Beneful Healthy Puppy) to support their energy and development.
