The **Working & Utility Breeds** section explores versatile, historically purpose-driven canines designed for specialized tasks—from herding and guarding to water-based labor and hunting. Highlighting breeds like the **Canaan Dog**, a low-maintenance, medium-sized guardian with ancient roots, alongside the **Portableuguese Water Dog**, a hypoallergenic, high-energy swimmer, and the **Irish Water Spaniel**, a web-footed, hypoallergenic hunter, this section emphasizes breeds built for endurance, adaptability, and unique functional traits. Ideal for active owners seeking both utility and companionship, these breeds blend practicality with distinctive physical and behavioral attributes.
