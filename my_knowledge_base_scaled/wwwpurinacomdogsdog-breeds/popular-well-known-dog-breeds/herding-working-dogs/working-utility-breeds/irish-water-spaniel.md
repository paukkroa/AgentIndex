# Irish Water Spaniel

The **Irish Water Spaniel** is a medium-to-large, high-energy dual-purpose hunting breed known for its intelligence, loyalty, and water-repellent, hypoallergenic coat (rich liver/dark puce liver). Males reach **22–24" (55–65 lbs)** and females **21–23" (45–58 lbs)**, with webbed feet enhancing swimming prowess. Bred for agility and obedience, they excel in activities like tracking, rally, and conformation. Requiring **regular exercise** and **occasional grooming**, this reserved yet affectionate breed thrives with active owners and benefits from joint-supportive diets (e.g., Purina ONE or Pro Plan).
