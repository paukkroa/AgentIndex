# Australian Cattle Dog

The **Australian Cattle Dog** is a medium-sized, high-energy herding breed developed in the 1800s in Australia, renowned for its intelligence, agility, and working prowess. Males stand **18–20 inches** tall and weigh **35–45 lbs**, while females are slightly smaller (17–19 inches). Their short, smooth coat comes in **red speckle or blue**, and they require minimal grooming but occasional brushing. Known for their loyalty and protective nature, they thrive in open spaces with structured activities like **agility, herding, obedience, and tracking**. Born completely white, they mature into robust, courageous dogs that excel in beef industry work. Owners must establish strong leadership due to their determined, pack-oriented temperament.
