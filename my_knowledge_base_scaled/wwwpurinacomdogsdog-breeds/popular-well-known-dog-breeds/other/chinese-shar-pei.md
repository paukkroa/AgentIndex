# Chinese Shar-Pei

The **Chinese Shar-Pei** is an ancient medium-sized breed (18–20" tall, 45–60 lbs) with a distinctive short, harsh coat in any solid color or sable. Originating in China over 2,000 years ago as a versatile farm dog, this breed is known for its intelligence, stubbornness, and adaptability to activities like agility, obedience, and tracking. While aloof with strangers, Shar-Peis form loyal bonds with their families. Their low-maintenance coat—often mistakenly called "sandpaper-like" (from the term *Shar-Pei*, meaning "sand skin")—requires minimal grooming. Early training and socialization are essential due to their independent nature. Ideal for owners seeking a medium-energy, quiet, and medium-shedding companion.
