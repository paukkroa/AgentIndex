# Clumber Spaniel

The **Clumber Spaniel** is a **medium-to-large, low-energy** breed originating in 18th-century France, renowned for its **gentle, loyal, and affectionate** temperament. Standing **17–20 inches tall** (males 18–20", females 17–19") and weighing **55–85 lbs**, this breed features a **dense, medium-length coat** primarily in **white with orange or lemon markings**. Known for its **slow, rolling gait** and **intelligence**, the Clumber excels in **hunting, agility, obedience, and tracking** but thrives on **daily walks** rather than strenuous exercise. Their **low-energy yet determined** nature makes them ideal for families and multi-pet households. Regular **daily brushing** is required to maintain their coat, while their **rare status in the U.S.** (despite early AKC recognition) highlights their niche appeal. Recommended products include **Pro Plan wet/dry formulas** for sensitive digestion.
