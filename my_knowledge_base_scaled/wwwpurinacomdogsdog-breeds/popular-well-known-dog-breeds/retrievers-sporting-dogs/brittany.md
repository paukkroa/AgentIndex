# Brittany

The **Brittany** is a medium-sized, high-energy sporting breed originating from the French province of Brittany, renowned for its exceptional hunting skills in pointing and retrieving game. Standing **17.5–20.5 inches tall** and weighing **30–40 pounds**, this agile, alert, and good-natured dog features a **feathered flat or wavy coat** in **orange-and-white or liver-and-white patterns**, requiring regular grooming to maintain its dense, field-protective texture. Known for its **high energy**, the Brittany thrives in active environments, excelling in **hunting, field trials, obedience, and conformation**. Historically documented in 18th-century French art, this breed combines athleticism with a **friendly, companionable temperament**, making it ideal for outdoor-loving families. Recommended products include high-protein dog foods to support its active lifestyle.
