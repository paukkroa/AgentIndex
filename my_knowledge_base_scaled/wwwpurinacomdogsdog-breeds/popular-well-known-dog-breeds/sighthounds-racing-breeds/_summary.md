The **Sighthounds & Racing Breeds** section explores two iconic athletic breeds—**English Greyhounds** and **Whippets**—known for their speed, elegance, and racing heritage. English Greyhounds, the fastest sighthounds, are large, low-maintenance dogs with moderate energy levels, ideal for active yet relaxed households. Whippets, slightly smaller and slightly slower (peaking at 35 mph), are equally low-maintenance with a gentle temperament and short coat, making them adaptable companions for both urban and rural living. This section highlights their distinct yet complementary traits, emphasizing their role as both elite racers and affectionate pets.
