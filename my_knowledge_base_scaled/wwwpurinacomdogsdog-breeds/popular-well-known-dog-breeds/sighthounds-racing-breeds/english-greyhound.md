# English Greyhound

The **English Greyhound** is a **large, ancient sighthound** renowned as the **fastest dog breed**, historically bred for hunting game like deer, foxes, and hares. Standing **25–30 inches tall** and weighing **60–70 lbs**, its **short, low-maintenance coat** comes in any color. Known for **medium energy**, it thrives on **daily exercise** (e.g., agility, running, lure coursing) and bonds closely with families and other dogs. Famous owners include **Presidents and generals**, reflecting its noble heritage. Recommended products include senior-specific dry foods (e.g., **Pro Plan or Purina ONE**) for joint and cognitive support. Explore similar breeds in **hound, medium/large, or low-shedding** categories.
