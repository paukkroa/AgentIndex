The **Toy & Small Companion Dogs** section explores a trio of distinctive breeds—each blending charm, functionality, and unique traits. The **Briard**, a towering herding giant with a protective instinct and high-maintenance coat, served as a WWI war dog. The **Poodle**, originating in Germany as a water retriever, stands out for its intelligence, adaptability, and modern roles in agility and companionship. The **Coton de Tulear**, a dainty, low-shedding white companion, offers a hypoallergenic, affectionate alternative for smaller spaces. Together, these breeds highlight diversity in size, purpose, and care, catering to active, intelligent, or low-allergen preferences.
