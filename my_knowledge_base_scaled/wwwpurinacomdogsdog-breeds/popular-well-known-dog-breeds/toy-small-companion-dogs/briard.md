# Briard

The **Briard** is a large, muscular herding breed originating from rural France, renowned for its loyalty, protective instincts, and strong memory. Standing **14–18 inches tall** and weighing **25–35 lbs**, this intelligent yet willful dog thrives in roles like herding, police/military work, search/rescue, and conformation. Its **distinctive appearance**—marked by bushy eyebrows, a shaggy beard, and a **long, wavy coat** in black, gray, or tawny—requires **daily grooming** to prevent matting. Historically, Briards served as **World War I French army dogs**, aiding in supply transport and battlefield rescue. Known for trainability and eagerness to please, they balance medium energy with a protective nature, making them versatile yet high-maintenance companions.
