# Coton de Tulear

The **Coton de Tulear** is a small, affectionate companion breed known for its distinctive **cotton-like, long white coat** and expressive, cheerful demeanor. Originating from Madagascar (formerly Tulear), this breed—nicknamed the *"royal dog of Madagascar"*—was historically favored by tribal nobility. Standing **9–11 inches tall** and weighing **8–15 lbs**, the Coton thrives on human interaction, making it an ideal indoor pet. Its **medium energy level** suits activities like agility, obedience, and tracking, while its **low-shedding coat** requires **weekly brushing** to maintain softness. Historically celebrated (even featured on Malagasy postage stamps), this breed blends charm, adaptability, and a playful personality, appealing to owners seeking a loyal, engaging small dog.
