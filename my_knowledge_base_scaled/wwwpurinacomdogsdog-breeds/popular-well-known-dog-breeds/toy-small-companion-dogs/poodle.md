# Poodle

The **Poodle** is a highly intelligent, versatile, and adaptable breed originating in **Germany** (not France) as a water retriever, now celebrated for its elegance and athleticism. Available in **three size varieties**—Toy (4–6 lbs, ≤10"), Miniature (10–15 lbs, 10–15"), and Standard (40–70 lbs, >15")—Poodles boast a **medium-length, low-shedding curly coat** in diverse colors (e.g., black, apricot, silver, cream). Known for their **medium energy**, they excel in **agility, obedience, and hunting tests**, thriving in active households with daily exercise. Their **hypoallergenic coat** (requiring **daily brushing and monthly trims**) and **10–18-year lifespan** make them ideal for allergy sufferers. While generally healthy, they may face **hip dysplasia, eye disorders, or epilepsy**, with smaller sizes prone to **Legg-Calvé-Perthes disease**.
