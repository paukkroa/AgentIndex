# Newest Dog Breeds

The **"Newest Dog Breeds"** page highlights contemporary canines developed within the last century, blending innovation with rarity. These breeds emerged from deliberate breeding efforts to refine traits like hunting ability (e.g., the **American English Coonhound**), temperament (e.g., the **Cesky Terrier**), or adaptability (e.g., the **Finnish Lapphund**). Some, like the **Parson Russell Terrier** and **Russell Terrier**, stem from selective lineage splits, while others, such as the **Vizsla**, showcase modern crossbreeding to preserve heritage traits. The page emphasizes their relatively recent recognition in kennel clubs and their distinct roles as working, companion, or rare pets, offering insights into their origins and specialized characteristics.
