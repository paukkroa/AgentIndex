# Types of Dog Breeds

This page categorizes **dog breeds by functional traits, temperament, and lifestyle compatibility**, offering structured navigation for informed pet selection. It highlights **five primary collections**—*Companion Dogs* (ideal for families, seniors, or active lifestyles), *Herding Breeds* (adaptable from farm work to urban roles, including small breeds like Corgis), *Hypoallergenic Breeds* (minimizing dander for allergy sufferers), *Long-Haired Breeds* (with grooming tips and hypoallergenic options), and *Low-Energy Breeds* (suited for low-activity households). Each section provides **detailed breed profiles** with expert insights on temperament, health, grooming, and care, alongside curated collections for traits like size or energy levels. The page emphasizes **personalized matching**—balancing activity needs, household dynamics, and allergies—to ensure a harmonious pet-human relationship.
